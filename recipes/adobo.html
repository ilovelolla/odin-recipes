Adobo

Craving a savory, tangy, and hearty dish? Try this Adobo-style Chicken – a delicious take on the Filipino favorite, perfect for any meal! Simmered in soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and spices, this dish bursts with rich flavors that deepen the longer it cooks. Serve it with steamed rice and taste the comforting essence of home in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken (cut into serving pieces)
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • head garlic (crushed)
  • 1 medium onion (sliced)
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp whole peppercorns
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar (optional)
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt
  • 1/3 cup vinegar

How to Cook Adobo-Style Chicken

  1. Heat oil in a pan, sauté garlic and onion until fragrant.
  2. Add chicken pieces, cook until lightly browned.
  3. Pour in soy sauce and vinegar (do not stir).
  4. Add bay leaves, peppercorns, and water.
  5. Let it boil, then lower heat and simmer for 30–40 minutes.
  6. Add sugar (optional) and season with salt to taste.
  7. Let sauce reduce until slightly thickened.
  8. Serve hot with steamed rice.
