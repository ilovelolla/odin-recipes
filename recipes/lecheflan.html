Leche Flan

your meal on a sweet note with Leche Flan — the creamy, velvety, and caramel-topped custard that's a staple in every Filipino celebration. Rich in flavor and smooth in texture, this classic dessert melts in your mouth and leaves you wanting more. It's the perfect blend of indulgence and tradition in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 10 egg yolks
  • 1 can (300 mL) condensed milk
  • 1 can (370 mL) evaporated milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup sugar (for caramel)

How to Make Leche Flan

  1. In a pan, melt sugar over low heat until golden (do not stir).
  2. Quickly pour and spread caramel into llanera or mold; let it set.
  3. In a bowl, gently whisk egg yolks, condensed milk, evaporated milk, and vanilla.
  4. Strain mixture to remove bubbles/lumps for a smoother texture.
  5. Pour into the caramel-lined mold and cover with foil.
  6. Steam for 30–40 minutes or bake in a water bath at 160°C (325°F) until set.
  7. Let cool, refrigerate for a few hours.
  8. To serve, run a knife around the edges and invert onto a plate.
